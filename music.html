Collabs I've done with musicians! Mostly of the visual variety. I've done music videos, album art, Spotify Canvas animations, music visualizers, and even live VJ performances! If you're a musician looking to collab please don't hesitate to email me.

Jaclyn Brockschmidt - Tell Me You Want Me
(Album Art, Spotify Canvas, Music Visualizers, 2024)

All of the materials for this album were shot on my massive Sony DV camcorder over the course of an evening. Still shots were taken to be glitched on my CRTs later for the album art. Once we had a look for the cover, I then took all of the footage we shot and edited it into distinct short loops for Spotify Canvas. I set those clips to loop on my CRT while the entire album played so I could capture audio reactive glitches for each song.

The full music visualizers didn't end up on youtube, but you can see some clips in the announcement promo video.

All my Spotify Canvas loops are visible if you check out the album on your phone!
