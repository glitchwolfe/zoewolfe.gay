WAVES

The following videos are all segments ("segs") I've done for wave movies.

Waves as I know them were started by Racer Trash in 2020. Basically, you chop a movie up into chunks, hand each part off to a different editor, and stitch them all back together exquisite corpse style.

Most of these features are ephemeral - they only exist once in a blue moon when screened on Twitch. I've left links to any projects that have full cuts available online, but for the most part this is just a list of my own segs.

Enjoy!!
